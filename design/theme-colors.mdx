Book Paradise establishes its premium identity through a carefully curated luxury palette — deep navy backgrounds, warm gold accents, and crisp white text — all wired up as CSS custom properties in `style.css`. Because every color across the site references these variables, you can retheme the entire storefront by editing a single `:root` block rather than hunting through hundreds of individual rules.

## Core Palette

The six core variables cover every color role in the design, from the darkest background to the brightest call-to-action:

| Variable | Value | Use |
|---|---|---|
| `--color-navy` | `#0a0f2c` | Primary background |
| `--color-gold` | `#d4af37` | Accents, headings, borders |
| `--color-white` | `#ffffff` | Body text, card backgrounds |
| `--color-cream` | `#f5f0e8` | Subtle backgrounds |
| `--color-accent` | `#ef7025` | CTA buttons, highlights |
| `--color-teal` | `#4fa1ab` | Links, secondary accents |

## Applying Custom Colors

All six variables live at the top of `style.css` inside the `:root` selector. To change any color across the entire site, update the corresponding line here — no other files need to be touched:

```css style.css
:root {
  --color-navy:  #0a0f2c;
  --color-gold:  #d4af37;   /* change this for a different accent */
  --color-white: #ffffff;
  --color-cream: #f5f0e8;
  --color-accent:#ef7025;
  --color-teal:  #4fa1ab;
}
```

<Tip>
  Changing `--color-gold` alone transforms the entire accent scheme throughout the site — every heading highlight, card border, scroll progress bar, and decorative separator picks up the new value automatically.
</Tip>

## Glassmorphism Cards

Several UI surfaces in Book Paradise — including feature cards and the cart sidebar header — use a glassmorphism treatment that layers translucency over the dark navy background. The effect is achieved with three properties working together:

- **Background:** `background: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.08)` — a barely-visible white tint that suggests a frosted surface without obscuring the backdrop.
- **Blur:** `backdrop-filter: blur(12px)` — softens whatever is behind the element, reinforcing the glass illusion.
- **Gold border:** `border: 1px solid rgba(212, 175, 55, 0.2)` — a subtle, semi-transparent gold outline that ties the card visually to the site's accent color.

Together these three declarations give the cards a sense of depth and luxury that flat or solid-color panels cannot achieve on the dark navy canvas.

<Note>
  The dark navy background (`#0a0f2c`) is intentionally very dark to maximize contrast with gold text and white body copy, meeting WCAG AA contrast ratio requirements. Avoid lightening `--color-navy` significantly without re-checking contrast ratios on all text elements.
</Note>
